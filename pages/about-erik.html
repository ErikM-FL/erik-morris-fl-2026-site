About Erik

Focused on practical solutions, accountability, and people‑first priorities.

This page summarizes the approach behind Erik Morris' 2026 write‑in campaign for Florida Governor and links to key priorities and resources across the site.

I have over 20 years of experience as a project manager, designer, creative and technical leader in themed and traditional entertainment. In 2021 I earned by PMP (Project Management Professional) certification. I have been part of multi-billion dollar projects across the globe with most of my work happening in the great state of Florida, where I was born and grew up. I am a native Floridian, born at Winter Park Memorial Hospital, and lived in Seminole County where I was part of the first ever class of 6th graders at Indian Trails Middle School. I graduated from Lyman High School, when the greyhound track across the street for which the school mascot was named, was still there. I moved to Tampa to go to school at the University of South Florida where I graduated with honors with a degree in Theatre Arts focusing on lighting and sound design.

Principles
